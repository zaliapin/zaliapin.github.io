Research Interests

Random self-similar trees; coalescent and branching processes; networks – structure and dynamics; multiscale methods of time series analysis; random sums of heavy-tailed variables; delay dynamical systems. Applications: statistical seismology; river networks and delta systems; climate stability; El-Niño modeling; stochastic dynamics of intracellular protein motors; financial stochastic modeling.
